Who We Are

Aurelian Jets is a private aviation brokerage providing access to on-demand charter and empty-leg flights through a network of FAA-certified Part 135 operators.

We manage the full charter process on behalf of our clients, from aircraft sourcing and operator vetting to scheduling, coordination, and ongoing trip support. Our role is to simplify private aviation while maintaining consistent standards across every flight.

We work with individuals, families, and organizations that require reliability, discretion, and clear communication when arranging private air travel.

02

Our Approach

  • Aircraft and operators are vetted prior to booking
  • Pricing is presented clearly before confirmation
  • Scheduling and coordination are handled end-to-end
  • Support remains available throughout the trip

Our focus is straightforward: dependable execution and a smooth experience from request to arrival.
