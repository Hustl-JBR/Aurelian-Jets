Private Jet Charter Brokerage

We arrange private aviation. You just fly.

Aurelian Jets is a charter broker — not an airline, not a marketplace. We work directly for you: sourcing aircraft from our network of vetted Part 135 operators, negotiating pricing on your behalf, and coordinating every detail of your trip from first inquiry to wheels down.

The broker model

Why use a broker instead of booking direct?

Operators own and fly the aircraft. Brokers — like Aurelian — represent you, the client. That distinction matters more than most people realize.

When you contact an operator directly, you're getting one company's fleet, their pricing, and their availability. When you work with us, we search the full market, verify each operator's safety credentials, compare aircraft for your specific route, and advocate for fair pricing on your behalf.

We've built the relationships so you don't have to. That means faster quotes, better options, and no surprises on the day of your flight.

What we do for every charter

  • 01
    Understand your trip We ask the right questions about timing, passengers, preferences, and flexibility — so we find the right aircraft, not just an available one.
  • 02
    Source and vet operators We only work with FAA Part 135 certified operators who meet our safety and service standards. Every operator is reviewed before they appear on a quote.
  • 03
    Present curated options You receive 2–4 aircraft recommendations with clear pricing, specs, and reasoning — no noise, no pressure.
  • 04
    Coordinate end-to-end Catering, FBO handling, ground transport, customs — we coordinate the details so you don't have to think about logistics.
  • 05
    Stay available throughout We're reachable before, during, and after your flight — for questions, changes, or anything that comes up.

Operator Vetting

Standards we hold every operator to

✓

FAA Part 135 Certification

Every operator must hold a current, active Part 135 air carrier certificate issued by the FAA. No exceptions.

✓

Third-Party Safety Ratings

We prioritize operators rated by ARGUS or Wyvern — independent safety audit programs that verify maintenance, crew, and operational standards.

✓

Liability Insurance

Minimum $100M liability coverage is confirmed and verified for every operator before a charter is arranged.

✓

Type-Rated Crew

Pilots must be type-rated on the specific aircraft they operate, with documented minimums in hours and experience.

✓

Maintenance Verification

Aircraft maintenance records and current airworthiness status are reviewed as part of our operator approval process.

✓

Operational History

We evaluate each operator's operational track record, incident history, and client feedback before adding them to our network.
